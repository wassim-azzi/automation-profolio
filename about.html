About Me

I hold a Licence (Bac+3) in Electronics, Electrical Engineering, and Automation (EEA). I am passionate about industrial automation, pneumatics, and electro-pneumatics. I have developed skills in designing pneumatic and electro-pneumatic systems using FluidSIM, programming Siemens PLCs with STEP 7, developing GRAFCET sequences, and simulating industrial automation systems. Motivated, detail-oriented, and eager to learn, I am looking to contribute to industrial automation and maintenance projects while continuing to expand my technical expertise.
